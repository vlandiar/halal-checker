How to Check Halal Status

Method 1: Official Halal Portal (Web)

  1. Visit myehalal.halal.gov.my
  2. Click on the search/verification section
  3. Enter the company, product, or restaurant name
  4. View the certification status and details

Method 2: Verify Halal Mobile App

  1. Download "Verify Halal" app from Google Play or App Store
  2. Open the app and tap the scan button
  3. Scan the product barcode or search by name
  4. Get instant verification from 60+ global certification bodies

Method 3: Look for Official Logo

Check for the official Malaysia Halal Logo on products and premises:

Green circular logo with crescent moon and star

Logo must include JAKIM or state religious authority name

About Halal Certification in Malaysia

  • JAKIM (Jabatan Kemajuan Islam Malaysia) is the main authority for halal certification in Malaysia
  • Halal certificates have expiry dates - always verify current status
  • State Islamic Religious Councils (like MAIS, MAINPP) can also issue halal certificates
  • Products without certification are not necessarily haram, but certification provides assurance
  • For inquiries, contact JAKIM at 03-8892 5000 or pr_halal@islam.gov.my
